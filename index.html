Frequently Asked

Is barter legal?

Yes! We're officially recognized under the Tax Equity & Fiscal Responsibility Act of 1982. We're classified as a third-party record keeperâ€”just like banks, brokers, and accountants.

How are taxes handled?

Trade dollars are treated exactly like cash for tax purposes. Sales become income, business expenses are deductible, and personal spending is taxable income. We provide all necessary tax documentation at year-end.

What about sales tax?

The seller collects sales tax on barter transactions just like cash sales. Buyers pay any applicable tax in cash (not trade dollars).

Who sets prices?

You do! Sellers trade at their full, fair retail prices. Our brokers monitor pricing to ensure fair trading policies across the network.
