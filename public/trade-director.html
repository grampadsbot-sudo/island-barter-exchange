Our Trade Director

Kimberly Giannini

Contact us at 650.533.5891

Kim Giannini-Davidson serves as Chief Trade Director of Island Barter Exchange, Inc., a premier barter community rooted in integrity, aloha spirit, and transformative opportunities for businesses and individuals across the islands and beyond.

Her journey into the world of barter began over 40 years ago in the vibrant streets of San Francisco, where she joined a boutique barter company as assistant to the CEO. With a modest salary supplemented by just $150 a month in trade dollars, she unlocked a world of luxury and convenience—dining at top restaurants, staying in elegant hotels, enjoying limo rides, and gifting family and friends unforgettable experiences like spa retreats and sunset cruises on the San Francisco Bay. That first taste of barter's magic hooked her for life.

Years later, after leading a successful executive headhunting firm for nearly two decades—following a tempting offer to relocate to Hong Kong with a banking chairman and his family—she rediscovered barter's power when reconnecting with her original mentor. Adding her family's vacation home in Hawaii to a trade portfolio opened doors to exceptional property management: skilled electricians, plumbers, designers, painters, house cleaners, and more—all traded seamlessly without draining cash reserves. The efficiency and joy it brought to managing multiple properties proved irresistible.

Inspired by countless guests, clients, and associates who expressed a desire for a fresh, values-driven barter network, Kim founded Island Barter Exchange, Inc.—a modern exchange dedicated to elevating lives through fair, trustworthy trade practices infused with genuine Hawaiian aloha.

"To create something exceptional, your mindset must be relentlessly focused on the smallest detail."

Much like her approach to curating upscale homes as owner and designer—where she focuses on the perfect match for exceptional living, lifestyles, and ideal tenants who treat each property as their own—Kim brings that same meticulous attention to barter.

At Island Barter Exchange, she invites you to discover the same life-enhancing freedom: stretch your resources, access premium services and experiences, build meaningful connections, and thrive in a community built on trust and mutual success. Join us!
