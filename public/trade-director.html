Our Trade Director

Kimberly Giannini

Contact us at 650.533.5891

Kimberly Giannini launched her barter journey many years ago in San Francisco, turning a modest $150 monthly trade allowance into fine dining, luxury hotel stays, limousine rides, and Bay sunset cruises that hooked her for life.

After running a successful executive headhunting firm for nearly two decades, she rediscovered barter's power by trading her Hawaii vacation home for premium property management services—electricians, plumbers, designers, painters, and cleaners—without touching cash, while also drawing inspiration from experiences and connections in Las Vegas.

Today, as Chief Trade Director of Island Barter Exchange, Inc., she builds a trustworthy, aloha-infused network that lets members access exceptional services, experiences, and connections while preserving cash flow and fostering genuine community.
